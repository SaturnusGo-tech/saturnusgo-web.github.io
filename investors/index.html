Investor overview

Infrastructure for intelligent mobility in emerging markets

We unify rides, bookings, payments, and loyalty — start where incumbents are weakest and compound with embedded finance.

A mobility & payments platform built as modular infrastructure. We start LATAM-first — launch in Argentina, expand across Brazil; next UAE; then EU. We unify fragmented ride and travel flows, and compound retention with embedded finance and loyalty.

“

One product spine across rides, bookings, and money — fewer hops, higher trust, better unit economics.

Why now

Mobility in emerging markets remains fragmented; payments are blocked or unreliable for many users. Super-app attempts bolt features; we ship a single spine that reduces cognitive load and unlocks spend.

What we build

Core ride ordering with class upgrades, wallet & transfers, hotel/event rails, and wishlists with AI planning. The infra is modular: each vertical can run standalone or compound.

How it works

Thin client over a service-oriented backend. Real-time via WebSocket; tokenized payments and balance. Cohort-based activation and loyalty to drive repeats.

Go-to-market

Start with Argentina city pairs; expand to Brazil with partner pilots. Subsequent rollouts target UAE, then EU. Focus on underserved segments and B2B2C funnels with compliance, risk, and reliable support.

Problem → Solution

Payments are blocked. Services are fragmented. Planning is manual.

SaturnusGo: enable cross-border payments, unify mobility + travel, add smart planning and financial intelligence.

Payments

Cross-border payments are broken (CIS → world)

Problem
  • Millions of CIS users are locked out of global mobility & travel.
  • Sanctioned cards, unsupported banks, and poor local adaptation block even basic transactions.
Solution
  • Localized, alternative, and sanctions-compliant rails for rides, hotels, and premium services.
  • Tokenized, multi-currency wallet with resilient routing and fallback methods.
Unification

Mobility is fragmented

Problem
  • Urban travelers juggle 3–5 separate apps for rides, hotels, planning, and payments.
  • The result: disconnected flows, wasted time, and broken journeys.
Solution
  • One platform unifying rides, bookings, wallet, and loyalty.
  • Consistent UX that reduces hops and lifts conversion.
Planning

Smart planning doesn’t exist

Problem
  • Short-distance getaways and spontaneous plans are manual and error-prone.
  • Users have to piece together routes, timing, and bookings across tools.
Solution
  • Built-in AI planning that adapts in real time (routes, timing, availability).
  • Weekend kits & wishlists that turn last-minute ideas into coordinated trips.
Realtime

Communication gaps kill experience

Problem
  • Driver–passenger comms are delayed, inconsistent, or vague.
  • No reliable status → stress, cancellations, and churn.
Solution
  • Real-time status via WebSocket (ETA, approach, pickup state).
  • Inline chat, safe-number calling, and smart nudges to prevent misses.
Finance

No financial intelligence

Problem
  • Users lack visibility and control across rides, hotels, and travel services.
  • Hard to track spend, optimize payments, or make informed choices.
Solution
  • Integrated financial view with category spend, limits, and controls.
  • Optimized payment routing and rewards to reduce effective cost.
Loyalty

Loyalty is broken

Problem
  • Every transaction is isolated; no cross-service perks or compounding value.
  • No incentive to stay within one ecosystem.
Solution
  • Unified loyalty across rides, hotels, and events.
  • Subscriptions & tiers that compound benefits and retention.
